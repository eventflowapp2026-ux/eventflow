About EventFlow

EventFlow is designed to simplify event registration and data collection while maintaining the highest standards of privacy, security, and ethical data usage. Our platform empowers organizers while protecting participants.

Our Data Flow Philosophy

Collection

Only collect necessary data with clear consent

2

Secure data with encryption and access controls

Respectful Use

Use data only for intended purposes with transparency

For Event Organizers

Your Responsibilities
Transparent Data Collection

Clearly state what data you're collecting and why. Never collect unnecessary personal information.

Secure Data Storage

Use EventFlow's built-in security features. Download data only when necessary and store it securely.

Respect Participant Privacy

Do not share participant data with third parties without explicit consent. Delete data when no longer needed.

Prohibited Activities
  • Creating forms for illegal or fraudulent activities
  • Collecting sensitive data (financial information, passwords, etc.)
  • Spamming or unsolicited marketing
  • Harassment or hate speech through forms
  • Impersonating others or organizations
Best Practices
  • Create forms with clear, concise questions
  • Use appropriate question types for the data needed
  • Set form schedules to prevent unwanted submissions
  • Regularly review and clean up old forms
  • Respond to participant inquiries promptly

For Form Participants

Your Rights & Safety
Privacy Rights
  • Know what data is being collected
  • Understand how your data will be used
  • Request data deletion from organizers
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
Safety Guidelines
  • Never share passwords or financial info
  • Be cautious with personal contact details
  • Verify the legitimacy of event organizers
  • Report suspicious forms immediately
When to Report a Form
Report forms if they:
  • Ask for sensitive personal information
  • Appear to be phishing attempts
  • Contain inappropriate content
  • Impersonate legitimate organizations
  • Violate these guidelines

Data Protection & Privacy

Encryption

Data transmitted and stored with encryption

Data Retention

Clear policies on data retention periods

Access Control

Restricted access to collected data

Compliance Standards
GDPR Principles Data Minimization Consent-Based Collection Transparency

EventFlow stores only the data necessary for form functionality:

  • Form responses (as provided by participants)
  • Organizer account information
  • Form metadata (titles, questions, settings)
  • Uploaded files (if enabled by organizer)

We do not store financial information, passwords, or unnecessary personal data.

Data retention depends on user activity:

  • Active forms: Data stored until form is deleted or closed
  • Inactive accounts: Data may be archived after 12 months of inactivity
  • Deleted forms: Data permanently removed within 30 days
  • User requests: Immediate deletion upon valid request

Consequences of Misuse

Immediate Actions
  • Form suspension or deletion
  • Temporary account suspension
  • Data removal for privacy violations
  • IP blocking for spam or abuse
Serious Violations
  • Permanent account termination
  • Legal reporting for illegal activities
  • Data preservation for investigations
  • Platform-wide IP bans
Reporting Violations

If you encounter misuse of the platform:

  1. Use the "Report Form" button on any suspicious form
  2. Contact support with detailed information
  3. Provide evidence (screenshots, URLs, etc.)
  4. Report legal violations to appropriate authorities

Our Commitment to Responsible Data Use

By using EventFlow, you agree to uphold these guidelines and contribute to a safe, respectful, and privacy-focused community for all users.
