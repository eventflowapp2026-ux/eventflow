Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

EventFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our event registration platform.

2. Information We Collect
A. Account Information
  • Registration Data: Username, email address, mobile number, password
  • Verification Data: OTP (One-Time Password) for email verification
  • Session Data: User ID, login status, and session preferences
B. Event and Form Data
  • Event Information: Event names, descriptions, categories, types, and notes
  • Form Content: Form titles, descriptions, questions, and configurations
  • Question Types: Text, radio, checkbox, dropdown, and file upload questions
  • Form Settings: Schedule settings (start/end dates), notification preferences
C. Form Response Data
  • Submission Data: Responses to form questions with timestamps
  • Uploaded Files: Documents, images, spreadsheets, presentations, archives (up to 100MB)
  • File Metadata: Filenames, file sizes, upload timestamps
  • Response IDs: Unique identifiers for each submission with QR codes
D. Communication Data
  • Email Lists: Recipient email addresses used for form invitations
  • Email Content: Custom messages sent with form invitations
  • Email Status: Delivery status, sent timestamps, and failure logs
  • Feedback: User feedback, ratings, and suggestions
E. Technical and Usage Data
  • IP Addresses: Real IP addresses with X-Forwarded-For header support
  • Device Information: Browser type, user agent strings
  • Usage Logs: Access times, page views, form submissions
  • Error Logs: System errors, email failures, and debug information
F. Moderation and Reporting Data
  • Form Reports: User reports of inappropriate forms with reasons and details
  • Block Data: Information about blocked forms, reasons, and durations
  • Moderation Logs: Admin actions on forms and user accounts
3. How We Use Your Information
  • Service Operation: Provide, operate, and maintain the EventFlow platform
  • User Accounts: Create and manage user accounts with email verification
  • Event Management: Create, edit, and manage events and registration forms
  • Form Processing: Collect, store, and display form responses
  • Email Communications: Send form invitations, notifications, and OTP codes
  • Scheduled Notifications: Send form end notifications based on schedule settings
  • QR Code Generation: Create QR codes for form sharing and response verification
  • File Management: Store and serve uploaded files with type and size validation
  • Content Moderation: Review reported forms and enforce community guidelines
  • System Monitoring: Monitor performance, track errors, and debug issues
  • Analytics: Generate statistics about users, events, forms, and submissions
  • Security: Prevent unauthorized access and protect against malicious activity
  • Compliance: Meet legal obligations and respond to lawful requests
4. Data Processing Roles

For Form Creators: EventFlow acts as a data processor for the data you collect through your forms. You are responsible for:

  • Complying with applicable data protection laws
  • Obtaining necessary consents from form respondents
  • Responding to data subject requests regarding collected data
  • Defining appropriate data retention periods

For Form Respondents: Your data is processed on behalf of the form creator. Questions regarding the use of your data should be directed to the form creator.

For All Users: EventFlow acts as a data controller for:

  • Account registration information
  • Usage and technical data
  • System logs and error reports
  • Communication preferences
5. Data Security Measures
  • Encryption: Data in transit protected with SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Authentication: Password hashing and session management
  • Access Controls: Role-based access for users, form creators, and admins
  • File Validation: Strict file type and size validation for uploads
  • Input Sanitization: Protection against SQL injection and XSS attacks
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute force attacks
  • Regular Backups: Automated backup system for data recovery
  • Security Monitoring: Logging and monitoring of security events

Note: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Users should avoid submitting highly sensitive information through the platform.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information in the following circumstances:

A. With Form Creators
  • Form responses submitted to their forms
  • Uploaded files from respondents
  • Response metadata and timestamps
B. With Service Providers
  • Email Services: SMTP providers for sending emails (Gmail)
  • Hosting Services: Cloud infrastructure providers
  • Analytics Services: Usage statistics and performance monitoring
C. Legal Requirements
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To respond to valid legal process (subpoenas, court orders)
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • To investigate potential violations of our Terms of Service
D. Business Transfers
  • In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • During due diligence processes

EventFlow does not sell personal information to third parties.

7. Data Retention
A. Account Data
  • Retained until account deletion or 2 years of inactivity
  • Users can request deletion via email
B. Event and Form Data
  • Retained until deleted by the creator
  • Automatic cleanup of orphaned data
C. Form Responses
  • Retained according to form creator's settings
  • Deleted when form or event is deleted
D. Uploaded Files
  • Stored in secure directories with access controls
  • Deleted when associated form or response is deleted
E. System Logs
  • Email logs: 90 days retention
  • Error logs: 30 days retention
  • Access logs: 60 days retention
8. User Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

A. Access and Portability
  • Request access to your personal data
  • Export your data in CSV format
  • View all data we hold about you
B. Correction and Deletion
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Delete individual form responses
C. Communication Preferences
  • Opt-out of non-essential emails
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Control email invitation settings
D. Account Controls
  • Close your account at any time
  • Download your data before deletion
  • Manage form access and sharing

To exercise these rights: Contact us at eventflow.app2026@gmail.com

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication: Session management and user login
  • Security: CSRF protection and form validation
  • Preferences: User interface preferences and settings
  • Analytics: Usage statistics and performance monitoring

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of the platform.

10. Children's Privacy

EventFlow is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
Important Security Notice: While we implement security measures, please avoid submitting:
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Financial information (bank details, card numbers)
  • Government-issued IDs (Aadhaar, PAN, passport)
  • Highly sensitive personal or medical information
