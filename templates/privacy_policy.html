Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

EventFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our event registration platform.

2. Information We Collect
A. Information You Provide
  • Account Information: Name, email address, mobile number, password
  • Event Data: Event names, descriptions, and form content
  • Form Responses: Information submitted by form respondents
  • Uploaded Files: Documents, images, or other files uploaded by users
  • Email Lists: Recipient email addresses used for invitations
B. Automatically Collected Information
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Cookies: Session cookies used for authentication
  • Log Data: Access times, pages viewed, and interaction data
  • Email Analytics: Delivery status, open rates, and click-through rates
3. How We Use Your Information
  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • Process and manage event registrations
  • Send email invitations and notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the Service
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations
4. Data Processing Roles

For Form Creators: EventFlow acts as a data processor. You are responsible for complying with applicable data protection laws for the data you collect.

For Form Respondents: Data is processed on behalf of the form creator. Questions regarding the use of such data should be directed to the form creator.

For Account Holders: EventFlow acts as a data controller for account-related information.

5. Sensitive Personal Information

EventFlow does not request, require, or encourage the submission of sensitive personal information through its platform.

Users are strictly advised not to submit or upload sensitive data, including but not limited to:

  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Bank account details, card numbers, or financial information
  • UPI IDs, PINs, or payment-related data
  • Government-issued identification such as Aadhaar, PAN, passport, or voter ID

Any sensitive personal information voluntarily shared through user-created forms is provided at the user's own discretion. EventFlow shall not be responsible or liable for the collection, disclosure, misuse, or loss of such information.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Email delivery, hosting, and analytics services
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition
  • Form Creators: Responses submitted to their forms

EventFlow does not sell personal information to third parties.

7. Data Security
  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure password storage using hashing
  • Access control and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security reviews and backups

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the Service or as required by applicable laws.

Form creators are responsible for defining retention periods for data collected through their forms.
